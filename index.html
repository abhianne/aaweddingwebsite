Our Love Story

6
Sept
2010

The First Meeting

Our love story began in September 2010 at University of Waterloo, through a Facebook friend request sent by the groom to the bride - Of course! Initially, we were just friends chatting on Facebook, met at university events and simply ran into each other in university hallways. However, we lost contact with each other and drifted apart as school workload became hectic overtime.

27
Jan
2013

The First Date

Fortunately, destiny made us cross paths again after 3 years and our love story was given a chance. It all happened when Abhishek boldly sent a Facebook message to Anne, wishing her Happy New year on January 1st, 2013 and wanting to reconnect with her again over dinner - Thanks to Mark Zuckerberg for Facebook. At the time, we were young and infatuated with each other inevitably so our early days flew by - a string of nonstop texts, long phone calls and constant flirtations being exchanged back and forth. It wasn’t until January 27th, 2013, we officially went on our first date.....and the rest was HISTORY!

28
Aug
2018

The Family Meeting

It was about time for our families to get to know each other and that was a real challenge, or so we thought. On August 28th, 2017, as nervous and stressful as we were, we arranged our parents to meet for the first time. To our delight, that meeting went better than we expected. They talked about how life was back home in India and Sri Lanka, their favourite South and North Indian dishes they like to cook, and their family's rituals and traditions. Overall, our parents incredibly bonded, gave us their blessings and accepted us into their families.

21
April
2018

The Proposal

As years passed by, our love for each other grew stronger that we couldn't wait any further, and decided to take our relationship to the next step - Marriage!. After getting the Bastiampillai’s permission to marry their daughter, Abhishek had been nonstop and constantly on his feet planning the perfect moment to propose to Anne. On April 21st, 2018, with the help of his close friends and a beautiful ring in hand, he got down on one knee, POPPED the big question and Anne said YES! Hooray!

23
June
2018

Engagement

On June 23rd, 2018, we officially announced our engagement and marriage to our family and friends, and celebrated our love with them. We are so excited and happy to begin the next chapter of our lives together and continue #AbhiAnneforever.
